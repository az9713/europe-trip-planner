Before leaving the U.S.
  • Confirm UK ETA approval for each traveler before boarding.
  • Enroll in STEP, save passport scans offline, confirm travel medical coverage.
  • Reserve Eurostar, timed museum entries, and at least three dinners before departure.
In London
  • Use the same contactless card for every TfL tap-in / tap-out.
  • Use black cabs, hotel-arranged cars, or licensed rideshare after late dinners.
  • Favor buses, river boats, taxis, and step-free stations with luggage.
In Paris
  • Phones zipped away near Gare du Nord, Eiffel Tower, Louvre, Sacré-Cœur, crowded Metro.
  • Ignore petition, bracelet, dropped-ring, and unsolicited-help approaches.
  • Use official taxi stands, G7, or hotel-booked taxis late at night.
